Let's Connect Know My Story

Who is Lalan Kumar?

I am an Electrical Engineering student pursuing my Diploma at Government Polytechnic Banka. Beyond being a student, I am a passionate developer, educator, and the driving force behind LK SMART STUDY and POLYTECHNIC HUB.

My journey bridges the gap between core engineering and modern web technology. I realized that polytechnic students lacked a centralized, premium platform for study materials, notes, and career guidance. That realization sparked the creation of this platform.

Mission

To provide free, high-quality technical education and resources to every polytechnic student.

Vision

To build India's most advanced digital ecosystem for diploma engineering students.

My Goals

  • Simplify complex engineering concepts.
  • Integrate AI for personalized study assistance.
  • Build a strong community of future engineers.

Future Plans

To expand POLYTECHNIC HUB to all states, develop mobile applications, and launch comprehensive placement preparation modules to ensure every diploma student lands their dream job.

The Founder's Story

My journey from a village student to a platform creator.

Village Background & Early Life

Coming from a humble village background, accessing quality education was always a challenge. The struggle to find proper guidance taught me the value of resources early in my student life.

The Polytechnic Journey

When I joined Government Polytechnic Banka to pursue Electrical Engineering, I noticed my peers facing the same struggles: scattered notes, confusing syllabus, and lack of mentorship.

Passion for Tech & Teaching

I have always had a deep passion for understanding how technology works. While studying electrical circuits, I started learning web development and AI on the side. I realized I could combine my technical skills with my desire to teach.

Creating POLYTECHNIC HUB

Under the umbrella of LK SMART STUDY, I began coding POLYTECHNIC HUB. It wasn't just about building a website; it was about building a lifeline for diploma students. Today, this platform is a step towards my future dream of revolutionizing technical education in India.
